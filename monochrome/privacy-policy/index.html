Monochrome Dome - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025-03-12


1. Introduction

Welcome to Monochrome Dome! This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you play our game.


2. No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data from users. Monochrome Dome is a completely offline, single-player game that does not require an internet connection to play.


3. Third-Party Services

Monochrome Dome does not use any third-party analytics, advertisements, or online services that collect user data.


4. Permissions

The game may request certain device permissions (e.g., storage access for saving progress), but this information stays on your device and is not transmitted anywhere.


5. Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, we will post the changes here.
